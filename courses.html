Courses!

Burlesque Fundamentals

We will be covering all burlesque fundamentals in the art of tease. With new choreography weekly.

Taming the Boa

We will be covering everything to do with boas from dance technique, care tricks, and purchasing tips. With new choreography weekly.

Feather Fan Dancing

Feather fan dancing is a popular prop in the art of tease. In this flirtatious showgirl camp you will learn how to style, add effortless elegance, and bring out the sexy side of every feather flutter. Learning a variety of poses, handling techniques, combos, feather fan care and other useful knowledge.

Private Lessons

What you want to learn in your private is up to you! Burlesque fundamentals, fans, boas, slow and sexy, comedy, flexibility, stretching, the business of burlesque, how to be more "bookable", how to approach producers, branding, costuming, act concepts, rhinestoning, skies the limit!
